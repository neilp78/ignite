f you haven’t started to self-isolate yet your isolation period began when you received your result and lasts for 7 days. 


If you begin to develop symptoms during those 7 days, you will need to isolate for a further 7 days since you started showing signs.</p>


You can stop self-isolation if you have been fever free for at least 2 days before the last day of isolation - if not, wait an additional 2 days. </p>


Please rest, drink plenty of water and take paracetamol to ease symptoms.</p>


You can ring NHS 111 at any time for information or advice and they will help you decide if you need to contact your GP. 

In addition people you live with should also self isolate for a period of 14 days.
