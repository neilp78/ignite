Tell us about your symptoms

Do you remember when you first noticed any symptoms?

For example, 12 11 2007

Do you remember the date when you started self-isolation?

If you haven’t started to self-isolate yet your isolation period began when you received your result and lasts for 7 days.

For example, 12 11 2007

You can stop self-isolation if you have been fever free for at least 2 days before the last day of isolation - if not, wait an additional 2 days.

Please rest, drink plenty of water and take paracetamol to ease symptoms.

You can ring NHS 111 at any time for information or advice and they will help you decide if you need to contact your GP.

In addition people you live with should also self isolate for a period of 14 days.
