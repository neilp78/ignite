Other situations you might have been in contact with other people

There might be other situations where you have been in contact with people for an extended piece of time where you might not know their details but it would still be helpful if you can provide us with any details you can remember.

Possible situations

Have you been in contact with somone in any of these scenarios (or similar) 48 hours before you felt unwell or received a positive test result?

  • A group of unidentified people (example lecture, public transport, other people you don’t know)
  • Deceased contact
  • Contact in ICU
  • A nursing home or a health / social care setting where a confirmed case is a patient / resident / member of staff
  • Contacts outside of Northern Ireland
